What's Included in Our AC Maintenance Service

A thorough AC service covers everything needed to keep your unit running at peak performance in Dubai's extreme climate

Filter Deep Cleaning

We remove, wash and dry all AC filters to remove accumulated dust, dirt and allergens. Clean filters improve airflow, cooling efficiency and indoor air quality significantly.

Coil Cleaning

Evaporator and condenser coils are cleaned using professional coil cleaners. Dirty coils reduce cooling capacity and increase electricity consumption.

Refrigerant Level Check

We check and top up refrigerant gas levels if needed. Low gas means poor cooling and higher electricity bills — we fix this during every maintenance visit.

Drain Pipe Cleaning

Blocked drain pipes cause water leaks and mold growth. We flush and clear all drainage pipes to prevent water damage to your walls, ceilings and furniture.

Electrical Connection Check

All electrical connections, capacitors and contactors are inspected for wear, corrosion and loose connections that can cause failures or safety hazards.

Full Performance Test

After service, we run a complete performance test to verify temperatures, airflow, noise levels, and thermostat accuracy before we leave your property.

Why Regular AC Maintenance is Essential in Dubai

Dubai's climate is one of the most demanding in the world for air conditioning. Summer temperatures regularly exceed 45°C, and the dusty desert environment clogs AC filters and coils much faster than in cooler countries. Without regular maintenance, your AC works harder, uses more electricity, and breaks down more often.

Our engineers recommend servicing your AC every 3–4 months in Dubai. At minimum, a thorough annual AC service before summer is essential to prepare your unit for the heavy cooling load ahead. Regular maintenance extends the lifespan of your AC by several years and can cut your electricity bill by up to 25%.
