Split AC installation in UAE starts from AED 200–400 depending on the location, pipe length needed, and complexity. We provide a free site survey and detailed quote before any work begins.

A standard split AC installation takes 2–4 hours. Central AC system installation for a villa or office can take 1–3 days depending on the size and complexity of the project.
