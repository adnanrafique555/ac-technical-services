How often should AC be serviced in Dubai?

Due to Dubai's extreme heat and high dust levels, we recommend servicing your AC every 2-3 months for optimal performance. Regular maintenance includes cleaning filters, checking refrigerant levels, and inspecting all components. This ensures efficient cooling, lower electricity bills, and extends the lifespan of your AC unit.

Yes! We offer 24/7 emergency AC repair services across Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Abu Dhabi, and Al Ain. Our technicians are available day and night to handle urgent cooling issues. Simply call us at +971 56 757 1868 or WhatsApp us anytime, and we'll dispatch a technician to your location promptly.

Do you repair refrigerators and freezers?

Absolutely! We specialize in refrigerator and freezer repairs for all major brands.
