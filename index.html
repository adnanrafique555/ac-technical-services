Frequently Asked Questions

How much does AC repair cost in Dubai?

AC repair costs in Dubai vary depending on the problem. Simple fixes like filter cleaning or sensor replacement start from AED 80–150. Gas refills typically cost AED 150–300. Compressor replacement is more expensive. We always give you a clear price before starting any work — no hidden charges.

How often should I service my AC in UAE?

In the UAE's dusty and hot climate, we recommend servicing your AC every 3–4 months. At minimum, get a full AC maintenance service once a year before summer. Regular servicing keeps your AC running efficiently, lowers electricity bills, and prevents costly breakdowns.

Do you repair all AC brands in UAE?

Yes — we service and repair all major AC brands available in the UAE including Samsung, LG, Daikin, Carrier, Gree, Midea, Hitachi, Panasonic, Toshiba, Haier, and more. Our technicians are trained on all brands and carry a wide stock of genuine spare parts.
