How often should my AC be serviced in Dubai?

In Dubai's extreme climate, we recommend servicing your AC at least twice a year — before summer (April) and after (October). Regular servicing prevents breakdowns during peak heat months, improves energy efficiency by up to 25%, and significantly extends unit lifespan.

Do you provide emergency AC repair services?

Yes — we operate 24 hours a day, 365 days a year including weekends and public holidays. Call or WhatsApp us and we will dispatch a certified technician to your location as quickly as possible, typically within 60–90 minutes in Dubai.

Do you repair refrigerators and freezers?

Yes, we repair all major brands of refrigerators and freezers — both residential and commercial. Our technicians handle compressor issues, thermostat failures, coolant leaks, ice build-up problems, and more, usually on the same visit.

Which UAE areas do you cover?

We provide full coverage across Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Abu Dhabi, and Al Ain. Our technicians are stationed strategically for fast response times. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, just call or WhatsApp us — we'll confirm immediately.

How much does AC service cost in Dubai?

Our pricing is fully transparent and competitive. We provide a free upfront quote before any work begins — no hidden charges ever. Costs depend on unit type, size, and the scope of work required. Call or message us for a fast, free quote.

Are your technicians licensed and insured?

Yes. All our technicians are fully certified, insured, and compliant with UAE safety regulations. Our company holds all necessary trade licenses to operate across the Emirates, giving you complete peace of mind on every job.
