Our AC Maintenance Service in Dubai

A comprehensive HVAC maintenance visit that covers everything needed to keep your AC running at peak performance in Dubai's extreme climate

Filter & Coil Cleaning

Deep cleaning of air filters, evaporator coils and condenser coils. Removes dust, dirt and allergens that reduce cooling efficiency in Dubai's dusty climate.

Gas Pressure Check

We check refrigerant gas pressure and top up if needed. Correct gas levels are essential for efficient cooling and preventing compressor damage.

Drain Pipe Clearing

Clearing blocked condensate drain pipes prevents water leakage and mold growth inside your AC unit and walls.

Electrical Inspection

Full check of all electrical connections, capacitors, contactors and control boards. Prevents electrical failures and fire hazards.

Fan & Blower Cleaning

Cleaning of indoor blower fan and outdoor condenser fan. Improves airflow, reduces noise and extends motor life.

Full System Report

After every maintenance visit, we provide a detailed report on your AC's condition and recommend any repairs needed before they become major problems.
